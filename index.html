James Reilly

Glasgow, Scotland, UK

James Reilly is a VP of Product Design with 20 years of experience building products in fintech, health, and fashion. He believes in strong foundations: clear information architecture, thoughtful interaction design, and systems thinking that scales.

His expertise encompasses the design of complex financial platforms, healthcare systems, and consumer applications. He has led design teams at multiple high-growth startups and established companies, shipping products used by millions of people.

Currently exploring how AI tools can accelerate design process without compromising craft. This site itself is an experiment in modern design workflow—built with AI assistance while maintaining editorial quality and accessibility standards.

Background

    • VP Product Design – JPMorgan Chase (2022–2024)
      Led IEP Insights platform for institutional clients, architected enterprise design systems
    • VP Product Design – Payroc (2020–2022)
      Built design team from ground up, shipped 4 major payment products in 18 months
    • Head of Design – ASOS (2018–2020)
      Led design for marketplace platform serving 20M+ customers across EU markets
    • Senior Product Designer – Publicis Sapient (2015–2018)
      Consulting across fintech clients including NatWest, Virgin Money, and Nationwide
    • Product Designer – Various Agencies (2011–2015)
      Specialized in financial services, healthcare systems, and consumer applications
    • Junior Designer – Design Studio (2005–2011)
      Foundation years learning craft across print, digital, and brand design
    • BA (Hons) Graphic Design – Glasgow School of Art (2001–2005)
      First Class Honours, specialized in typography and information design
    • Continuing Education
      Nielsen Norman Group UX Certification, Design Leadership workshops, regular conference attendance (UXDX, Leading Design)

  • Design: Figma (expert), Sketch, Adobe Creative Suite, Prototyping (Principle, ProtoPie), Design Systems, Component Libraries

    Process: User Research, Information Architecture, Interaction Design, Usability Testing, A/B Testing, Analytics (Mixpanel, Amplitude)

    Technical: HTML/CSS (proficient), Basic JavaScript, Git, Design Tokens, Accessibility (WCAG 2.1), Responsive Design

    Leadership: Team Building, Hiring, Mentoring, Design Critiques, Stakeholder Management, Cross-functional Collaboration
